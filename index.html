Why you'll love Litnerd_letters 💜

User-Friendly Editor 📝

This platform offers a user-friendly editor that makes creating and formatting blog posts a breeze. With rich text options, image uploads, and easy-to-use tools, you can focus on your content without worrying about the technicalities.

Personalized Feed 📰

Stay up-to-date with the latest content that matters to you. The site's personalized feed curates blog posts based on your interests and reading history, ensuring you never miss out on relevant articles.

Categories Navigation 📱

Navigate through various blog categories such as Science, Technology, Health, Lifestyle, and Fashion with more coming soon.

About Litnerd letters

This project was inspired by one of my friends who is a medical student and likes writing articles.He earlier inquired from me how he can create a website to write and post his articles in. I recommended an online web builder. I purposed to build my own custom version of the same when I learned the skills to do so.So the naming of the project and the idea generally came from here.

I'm slowly adding features to the site before fully deploying it. And maybe later, I will provide him an avenue to write and share his articles, plus many other people. I leveraged this opportunity to learn as I apply the skills in this project. I'm slowly developing it and adding several functionalities, making sure it is excellent.
